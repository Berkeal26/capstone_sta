Test 2: Barcelona Itinerary

Day 1: Explore the Gothic Quarter

  • Start your day with a visit to the iconic Sagrada Familia.
  • Wander through the charming streets of the Gothic Quarter, visit the Barcelona Cathedral, and explore the historic Plaça del Rei.
  • In the afternoon, visit the Picasso Museum and take a stroll down La Rambla.

Day 2: Gaudi Tour

  • Begin your day with a visit to Park Güell to admire the stunning mosaics and architecture of Antoni Gaudí.
  • Head to Casa Milà (La Pedrera) to explore another one of Gaudí's masterpieces.
  • Visit Casa Batlló, another one of Gaudí's famous works.

Day 3: Montjuïc and Beach Day

  • Start your day by taking a cable car to the top of Montjuïc Hill to enjoy panoramic views of the city.
  • Visit the Montjuïc Castle and explore the Montjuïc Gardens.
  • Head to Barceloneta Beach for some relaxation and sunbathing.
